Support

Help for subscriptions, access, and app questions.

Use this page if you need help with Swing Thoughts, including subscription access, restoring purchases, or general support.

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Contact

For support, contact remy.nts@gmail.com.

When possible, include your device model, OS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

2. Subscription help

Swing Thoughts requires a Premium subscription to use the app. Subscription billing, renewals, cancellations, and refunds are handled by Apple or Google through the store account used for the purchase.

  • To manage or cancel a subscription, use your store account settings.
  • To restore access on the same store account, open the app and use Restore Purchases on the paywall.
  • If access does not return after restore, contact support with your device and purchase details.

3. Troubleshooting

  • If the paywall does not load, check your internet connection and reopen the app.
  • If purchases are not recognized, try Restore Purchases first.
  • If the app crashes or behaves unexpectedly, send the device model, OS version, and the action that triggered the issue.
