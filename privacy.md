Privacy Policy – Swing Thoughts

Swing Thoughts helps users save golf thoughts, journal entries, photos, videos, and resource links.

Data stored on device:
Most user content, including thoughts, journal entries, photos, videos, and app preferences, is stored locally on the user’s device.

Data shared with third parties:
- Analytics data may be processed by Amplitude to understand app usage and improve the product.
- Crash and diagnostic data may be processed by Sentry to detect and fix technical issues.
- Subscription and purchase-related data may be processed by RevenueCat and Apple to manage premium access.
- When a user adds certain external links, the app may request preview metadata from the corresponding provider.

We do not sell personal data and we do not use data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

Users can contact us to ask privacy-related questions or request support at: saxotools@gmail.com
